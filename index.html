FAQ

Questions you probably have

Everything you need to know before we work together.

How long does it take to build my website?
+

Starter sites are delivered in 2 weeks, Growth sites in 3 weeks, and Premium stores in 4 weeks. This includes design, development, content integration, and testing across all devices. I never rush — quality comes first.

What do I need to provide to get started?
+

Just your business details, logo (if you have one), any photos you want to use, and your goals. I'll handle everything else — design, writing, images, and development. If you don't have a logo, I can create one for you.

Will my website work on mobile phones?
+

Absolutely — every site I build is mobile-first. That means I design for mobile first, then scale up to desktop. With 70%+ of traffic coming from phones in India, this is non-negotiable for every project.

Do you offer payment in installments?
+

Yes! I take 50% upfront to begin the project, and the remaining 50% only when you're happy with the final result and we go live. All payments via UPI, bank transfer, or Razorpay.

Can I update the website myself after launch?
+

Yes! I provide full handover training after launch so you can update text, images, and products yourself. All packages include 1-3 months of free support, and I'm always just a WhatsApp message away.

Will my website show up on Google?
+

Every site I build includes basic SEO — proper page titles, meta descriptions, fast loading speed, and Google Search Console setup. Growth and Premium packages include advanced SEO to help you rank for local searches in your city.

What if I'm not happy with the design?
+

No problem at all — every package includes 2 full revision rounds. I won't move to development until you're 100% satisfied with the design. Your happiness is the only deadline that matters.

Do you work with clients outside Amravati?
+

Yes! I work with clients across India — 100% remotely via WhatsApp, calls, and email. The entire process from discovery to launch can be done without a single in-person meeting.

Still have questions? I'm happy to answer anything.
